Chat live with your website's visitors in realtime with WalkyTalky (aka Walky Talky).

== Description ==

With WalkyTalky (aka Walky Talky), you can easily interact with your website's visitors. Chat live with them to answer any questions
they may have and assist them as they browse your website.

This official plugin makes it simple to add the WalkyTalky chat widget onto your website with a click of a button.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I use WalkyTalky for free? =

Yes, the basic chat facility on WalkyTalky is free to use.

= What do I need to use this plugin? =

You will need a WalkyTalky account and have already added your site to WalkyTalky. You will then need to enter your
site's ID to activate the plugin.
